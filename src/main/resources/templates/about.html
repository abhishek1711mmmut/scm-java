About Us

What is SCM?

Smart Contact Management (SCM) is an intuitive web application designed to help users manage their contacts efficiently. Whether you need to add, edit, or delete contacts, SCM provides a seamless experience with an easy-to-use interface.

Key Features

  • Secure user authentication and registration
  • Add, edit, and delete contacts with ease
  • Beautiful user interface with light and dark modes
  • Responsive and mobile-friendly design

Our Mission

Our goal is to simplify contact management for individuals and businesses, ensuring data is easily accessible and well-organized. SCM is built with security, user-friendliness, and efficiency in mind.
